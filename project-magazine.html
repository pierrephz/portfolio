Magazine Paris Fantastique.

Big scale graphism – 50 000 magazines released in China and Paris.

During my internship at centdegrés in Shanghai, I was asked to design a new magazine: "Paris Fantastique". This magazine is aimed at the rich Chinese living in Paris. In this magazine, you will find all the information on the French capital's luxury brands, luxury hotels, restaurants and historical monuments. But also, at each edition, a focus on a region of the country. I received all the articles translated into Chinese, pictures from photographers. My mission was to design the entire magazine, from scratch. The first one I did during the internship was 90 pages. The second one, made for them as a freelancer after completing my internship, was 120 pages long.

Skills

• Freelance – Manage my work and deal with clients
• InDesign – Huge content and export for a large scale

Ce projet m'a fait grandir et fait découvir le monde de la freelance. Taught me to work under pressure, solve last minute problems, receive feedback and make changes every day, export work to print on a large scale (50,000 copies). I also had to adapt to a language that is not mine, and design a magazine for a very specific community: Chinese people living in France.
