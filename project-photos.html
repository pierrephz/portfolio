On the plane, flying back

Photography – Nothing is more important than a camera when you travel.

"On the return flight" is a serie of picture from my past travels.
The idea here is to document every trip, and make one image representing the feeling and the mood I had experienced once there.
In the plane, on the train, on my way back, my main occupation is checking my picture, select them, edit them ans save them.

Skills

• Photography – camera settings, sense of the shot
• Open mind – Capture the good and relevant subject on the place you are in
